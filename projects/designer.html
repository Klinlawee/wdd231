Designer Profile

Morrison Koffie

With over 15 years of experience in the fashion industry, Morrison has established herself as a visionary designer known for her impeccable attention to detail and innovative use of textiles.

After graduating from the prestigious Central Saint Martins in London, Morrison worked with several luxury fashion houses before launching her eponymous label in 2015. Her designs have been featured in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and Elle.

Morrison's design philosophy centers around sustainable luxury, combining traditional craftsmanship with contemporary aesthetics to create pieces that stand the test of time.
