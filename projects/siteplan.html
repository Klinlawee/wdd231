National Parks Explorer - Site Plan

1. Site Name

Name: National Parks Explorer

Reason: The name clearly indicates the site's purpose - helping users explore and learn about U.S. national parks. It is simple, memorable, and SEO-friendly.

Optional Domain: nationalparksexplorer.org (available for registration)

2. Site Purpose

The website serves as an informational hub for national park enthusiasts, offering:

  • Park Directory: A dynamic list of national parks with key details (location, activities, weather).
  • Conservation Awareness: Information on preserving parks and responsible tourism.
  • Trip Planning: A form for users to request park guides and additional resources.

3. Scenarios

  1. "Which national parks are best for hiking, and what trails do they offer?"
    • The site will provide park details, including popular trails and difficulty levels.
  2. "How can I support national park conservation efforts?"
    • The "About" page will include volunteer programs and donation links.

Font Category Usage

6. Wireframe

Mobile View (320px)

Header: Logo + Hamburger Menu
Hero Image + Call-to-Action Button

Desktop View (1200px+)

Header: Logo + Horizontal Navigation
Hero Image with Overlay Text

Footer: Quick Links, Contact Form, Attribution
